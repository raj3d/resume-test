About Me

Motion. Depth. Storytelling.

I am a 3D Animation & VFX Artist with a passion for breathing life into imaginative worlds through meticulous craft and technical artistry. My work spans character animation, visual effects, motion graphics, and cinematic rendering — each project a new canvas for visual storytelling.

From pre-visualization to final compositing, I handle the full pipeline with a director's eye for detail and an animator's feel for timing. I collaborate closely with directors, producers, and creative teams to ensure every frame serves the story.

I believe great VFX is invisible — it serves the narrative so seamlessly that the audience never notices the craft, only feels its impact.
